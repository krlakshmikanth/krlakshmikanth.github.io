Yesterday’s “Gemini at Work Live” event showcased Google’s latest AI advancements, emphasizing how data is crucial for unlocking AI’s potential. While “foundation models” are powerful, the event stressed that they alone aren’t enough. Businesses need a holistic strategy to prepare data, choose the right models, and seamlessly integrate them into their systems. This is where Google’s offerings shine.

Gemini: More Than Just Models

The event highlighted Gemini’s capabilities beyond basic AI models. Here are some key takeaways:

Customer-Centric Solutions: Gemini is revolutionising customer service with intelligent agents. Imagine agents that not only understand complex requests but also provide personalised experiences.

Boosting Workplace Efficiency: Gemini is set to redefine productivity. Employee agents will streamline tasks, offer instant information access, and make workflows incredibly smooth.

Data at Your Fingertips: Features like Vertex AI Search, powered by Google’s renowned search technology, will allow businesses to extract invaluable insights from their data.

Looker Gets Smarter: With Gemini, Looker becomes even more powerful. Conversational analytics allows users to interact with data using natural language, get AI-driven suggestions, and visualise insights effortlessly.

BigQuery: The Data Powerhouse

The event positioned BigQuery as the cornerstone of this AI revolution. Here’s why:

Unified Engine: BigQuery’s new unified engine ensures high performance and seamless management, making it ideal for handling the demands of AI workloads.

Unified Storage: BigQuery is designed to handle various data types (multimodal data), a crucial aspect of today’s diverse data landscape.

Built-in AI: With BigQuery ML and other integration capabilities, BigQuery emerges as a comprehensive platform for AI solutions.

Finally Google’s AI Advantage

The event strongly emphasized Google Cloud’s unified tech stack as a significant advantage.

AI Hypercomputer: This forms the foundation for Google’s AI prowess.

Vertex AI: This serves as the platform for building and deploying AI models, including the powerful Model Garden.

Gemini: This sits at the forefront, powering Google Cloud, Workspace, and specialised Agents.

The event presented a compelling case for businesses to view their data as a competitive advantage in the age of AI. Google Cloud, with its suite of integrated solutions like Gemini, Vertex AI, and BigQuery, offers a comprehensive pathway for businesses to harness their data’s full potential.
